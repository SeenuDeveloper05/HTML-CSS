About School

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is the primary setting for much of the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling. It is a magical boarding school in Britain where young witches and wizards are trained in the magical arts.

Harry potter

Harry Potter is the central character of the Harry Potter movie series, portrayed by actor Daniel Radcliffe. Orphaned as a baby after the dark wizard Lord Voldemort murdered his parents, Harry was raised in the non-magical world by his unkind relatives, the Dursleys. On his 11th birthday, he discovers that he is a wizard and receives an invitation to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

Hermoine

Hermione Granger, played by Emma Watson, is one of the main characters in the Harry Potter movie series and the best friend of Harry Potter and Ron Weasley. She is known for her intelligence, strong work ethic, and deep loyalty. Born to Muggle (non-magical) parents, Hermione proves that magical ability is not limited to bloodline, as she consistently excels in her studies at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where she is sorted into Gryffindor House

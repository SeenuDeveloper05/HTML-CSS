About Sk inventa

Online shopping has become increasingly popular due to its convenience and flexibility. It allows people to purchase products anytime and from anywhere without the need to visit a physical store. One of the biggest advantages is the ability to compare prices easily and find better deals and discounts, often more than what traditional shops offer. Online platforms also provide a wider variety of products, including access to international brands and rare items. Home delivery services save time and effort, especially when ordering large or heavy goods. Customers benefit from reading real user reviews and ratings, which help them make smarter buying decisions. Additionally, online shopping offers multiple secure payment options like UPI, credit/debit cards, and even cash on delivery. All these features make online shopping a time-saving, cost-effective, and user-friendly option for modern consumers.

Flipkart

Flipkart is one of India's leading e-commerce companies, founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. It started as an online bookstore but quickly expanded into selling electronics, fashion, home goods, and more. Flipkart became very popular for its wide range of products, affordable prices, and fast delivery services. In 2018, it was acquired by Walmart, which helped it grow even further. Today, Flipkart is a trusted platform used by millions of customers across India.

Amazon

Amazon is a leading global e-commerce website that allows people to buy and sell a wide range of products online. Founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, it started as an online bookstore and quickly expanded into electronics, clothing, home goods, and more. Today, Amazon is known for its fast delivery, customer-friendly service, and secure payment system, making it one of the most trusted and convenient online shopping platforms in the world.

Meesho

Meesho is a popular Indian e-commerce platform that enables individuals, especially small business owners and resellers, to sell products through social media platforms like WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram. Founded in 2015, Meesho offers a wide range of affordable products such as clothing, home essentials, and beauty items. It empowers users to start their own online business with zero investment, making it a unique and accessible shopping and reselling platform in India.
